Bayesian Model

Each shot updates two conjugate Gaussian priors:

  • Model 1 — Grind→Time: slope prior μ=−2.5 s/step, σ²=1.5, noise σ²=4.0. Predicts the grind setting needed for a 30 s shot.
  • Model 2 — Grind Time→Dose: slope prior μ=1.8 g/s, σ²=0.5. Predicts dose from grind duration.

After each shot the posteriors are updated via: σ²ₙ = 1/(1/σ²₀ + Σxᵢ²/σ²ₙₒᵢₛₑ), then the slope mean is adjusted towards observed data. Confidence grows as shots accumulate.

Roast offsets shift the initial prior: Light +2 steps coarser, Dark −2 steps finer.
