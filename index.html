Bayesian Model

How the app learns

The grinder model starts with a prior that finer settings slow shots by about 2.5s per step. Each logged shot updates that belief with closed-form normal-normal posterior math, then projects the grind change needed to land near a 30s shot.

The dose model does the same for grind time, learning how many grams per second your grinder is delivering so the next recommendation nudges grind time toward the selected basket's target dose.
